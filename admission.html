Entry Requirements

Applicants must be of the appropriate age for the program they are applying to. Please prepare the following:

  • Birth certificate or proof of age
  • Immunization record
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Completed application form
  • Two passport-size photographs

How to Apply

1
Download the application form

Get the latest form below and read the instructions carefully.

2
Prepare required documents

Attach copies of birth certificate, immunization record, and recent photos.

3
Submit your application

Submit at our office during working hours or email a scanned copy to upskillacademy81@gmail.com.

4
Attend an interview/orientation

We will contact you to schedule a short meeting and school tour.
