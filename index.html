Historical Background

Up Skill Academy was founded in 2010 with the vision to provide quality education and foster creativity among young learners. Over the years, the school has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development. Our journey began as a small daycare center and has expanded into a full primary school serving families across Maungani and surrounding communities.

Key milestones include the opening of our nursery program in 2012, the addition of a primary curriculum in 2015, and the launch of community outreach and enrichment programs in 2018. We continue to invest in teacher training, safe facilities, and play-based learning materials to support every child's growth.
