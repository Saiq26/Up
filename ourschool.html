Our Programs

Daycare, Nursery and Primary education designed to build confident, curious learners.

Daycare

Age: 6 months - 2 years

Hours: 7:30 - 16:00

Our daycare program focuses on nurturing routines, safe play, supervised naps and nutritious meals. Caregivers use play-based learning and gentle routines to support early development.

Enrollment: Contact admissions or visit the school to register.

Nursery

Age: 3 - 4 years

Hours: 8:00 - 15:00

Nursery focuses on early literacy, numeracy readiness, social skills and creative play. Our classrooms are bright and resource-rich with trained early-years educators.

Curriculum highlights: Story time, phonics play, sensory activities, outdoor play and basic numeracy.

Primary School

Age: 5 - 11 years

Hours: 8:00 - 15:30

Our Primary program blends a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy with creative subjects, ICT basics and co-curricular activities. Emphasis is on safe learning, child-centred instruction and skill-building.

Assessment & progress: regular formative checks, end-of-term reports and parent meetings.
